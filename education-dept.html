About the Department

The Department of Education is committed to preparing competent, innovative, and ethical educators for the 21st century. We offer a range of programmes that blend theory with practice, ensuring our graduates are ready to lead in classrooms and educational institutions across Kenya and beyond.

Programmes Offered

Undergraduate Programmes

  • Bachelor of Education (Arts)
  • Bachelor of Education (Science)
  • Bachelor of Education in Early Childhood

Postgraduate Programmes

  • PhD in Educational Administration and Planning
  • PhD in Educational Psychology
  • Master of Education in Early Childhood Education
  • Master of Educational Psychology
  • Master of Education in Guidance and Counselling

Admission Requirements

KCSE C+ or C (Plain) with Diploma in a relevant field.
