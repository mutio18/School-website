About Us

Our history, mission and commitment to excellence

Makueni University College is a growing institution dedicated to academic excellence, research, and community development. As a constituent college, we are committed to expanding access to higher education while nurturing innovation, integrity, and leadership among our students.

We offer a range of academic programs designed to equip learners with relevant skills for today’s dynamic world. Our commitment to quality teaching, modern learning approaches, and strong partnerships ensures that every student receives a transformative educational experience.

Makueni University College continues to build a reputation as a center of knowledge, character formation, and sustainable development within Makueni County and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide transformative education that nurtures integrity, innovation and academic excellence while empowering students to contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To become a leading institution recognized for producing skilled, ethical and globally competitive graduates who drive sustainable development.

Our Values

We uphold professionalism, respect, accountability and a commitment to continuous improvement in all aspects of learning and service.
