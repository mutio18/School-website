Admissions

Your pathway to joining Makueni University College

Makueni University College welcomes applicants from diverse backgrounds who are eager to pursue academic excellence and personal growth. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, supportive, and accessible to all prospective students.

Whether you are a first‑time applicant, a transfer student, or an international learner, our admissions office is committed to guiding you through every step of the process.

Explore the sections under Admissions to learn more about application procedures, available programmes, registration steps, payment methods, and requirements for international students.
